### MathShot! the Company

MathShot! was developed at Georgia Tech as part of the Institute entrepreneurship and innovation
initiative. Our mission is to leverage advanced technology and sophisticated algorithms in order
to make it possible for anyone to do math without even thinking.

Our product will help to make
math classes obsolete. In this way, we expect to be at the forefront
of the inevitable revolution in science and engineering education.
