# Georgia Tech Announces Adoption of MathShot!

Posted April 1, 2012 **Atlanta, GA**

### For More Information Contact

Dr. Loof Lirpa, President

MathShot! Software

info@mathshot.com

  Tech TowerDownload image

The Georgia
Institute of Technology is pleased to announce that, starting in Fall 2012, we will be providing every student with a free copy of MathShot!

With MathShot!, students can simply take a picture of the math problem they want to solve. Then they click the "Solve" button and get their answer!

*“It is critical for engineering and science students to be able to compute the solution to mathematics problems quickly and accurately. We have done rigorous testing with MathShot! and it can handle any mathematics problem that an undergraduate student is likely to come across.”*
 
                   
                   -
Dr. Loof Lirpa, President of MathShot! Software

Since MathShot! can handle any undergraduate level math problem
at the click of a button, Georgia Tech also expects to **reduce the size of their mathematics department by more than 50%**. This will result in a huge savings because undergraduate students will no longer need to take mathematics classes.
