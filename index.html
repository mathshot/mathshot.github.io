How Does it Work?

Simply take a picture of the math problem you want to solve.  Click the "Solve" button and there's your answer!

See screenshots on our products page.

MathShot! uses advanced character recognition to convert math problems into symbolic expressions in a formal system.  The solution is then computed and displayed to the screen.
