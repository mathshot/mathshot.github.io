### How Does it Work?

Simply take a picture of the math problem you want to solve. 
Click the "Solve" button and there's your answer!

See screenshots on our products page.

MathShot! uses advanced character recognition to convert math
problems into symbolic expressions in a formal system.  The
solution is then computed and displayed to the screen.

### Availability

MathShot! is available for any Android or iOS device, and will run on Windows 8.

MathShot! is completely legal in 49 states and Guam, but for some weird reason there
is a legal challenge in North Dakota so it cannot be distributed there. That should
only be a problem for one or two people but we have to mention it.

The app is available for free download to authorized Georgia Tech students.  
Outside of Georgia Tech it is $10.  
We are also developing *MathShot! Lite* which will have a
simpler interface for students at non-engineering schools like the University of Georgia.
